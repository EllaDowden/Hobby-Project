Dungeons & Dragons

D&D is an exciting fantasy role-playing game!

Dungeons and Dragons is a fantasy role-playing game first published in 1974 by designers Gary Gygax and David Arneson. Players role-play adventuring characters with different races and classes such as an elf wizard or dwarf fighter. One player, the Dungeon Master, facilitates the game, controls the monsters and non-player characters, and narrates the story. All players must work together to move the story forward, completing quests and slaying mosters.

Learn more on the Wizards of the Coast website!

These are just a few examples of the sort of characters you could create:

  • A rogue who uses stealth and trickery to overcome enemies
  • A wizard capable of manipulating magic through their intellectual prowess
  • A fighter with mastery of martial combat and imense strength
  • A cleric who wields divine magic in service of a higher power
  • A bard who excels in charisma and uses the magic of music
